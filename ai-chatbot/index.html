01 — Context

Problem & Objectives

Challenges
  • Fragmented support across chat, email, forms
  • Manual workflows across disconnected systems
  • Inconsistent response quality at scale
  • Agents searching ERP, CRM, order systems manually
  • Customer satisfaction declining under volume
Goals
  • Unified AI-driven routing across all channels
  • Automated tool invocation (ERP, CRM, couriers)
  • Policy-based workflow execution
  • Validated, structured outputs with guardrails
  • Production observability via Grafana

10 — Delivery Approach

Client Engagement & Delivery

🔍

Channel Mapping

Mapped chat, email, form, voice touchpoints. Identified 15+ intent categories with the support team lead.

📝

Single-Channel POC

Prototyped live chat first. Validated routing accuracy and tone before expanding to email and forms.

👥

Workshops with Ops

2-hour sessions with support leads to define escalation thresholds, policy rules, and response tone.

🚀

Store-by-Store Rollout

Deployed to 1 store, validated for 2 weeks, then scaled to all 5 stores with env promotion.

📈

Grafana Feedback Loops

Weekly review of resolution rate, escalation trends, and CSAT. Drove prompt and routing refinements.

💬

Exec Reporting

Monthly trade-off presentations to non-technical stakeholders on cost, quality, and automation rate.
