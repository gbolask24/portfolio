01 — Context

Problem & Objectives

Challenges
  • Supplier data inconsistent and unstructured
  • Manual product creation slow and error-prone
  • Content lacks SEO structure and consistency
  • Catalogue stuck at 12k SKUs, limiting revenue
  • No scalable pipeline from supplier to storefront
Goals
  • Config-driven ingestion from multiple sources
  • Multi-agent content generation pipeline
  • Pinecone RAG for taxonomy classification
  • Schema-validated structured output
  • Direct publishing to E-commerce and ERP

11 — Delivery Approach

Client Engagement & Delivery

🔍

Workflow Audit

Shadowed content team creating products manually. Timed each step. Identified 90% automation potential.

📝

Single-Category Pilot

Ran downlights category first (200 SKUs). Validated attribute accuracy before expanding scope.

👥

Schema Co-Design

Merchandising team defined quality standards. Shared attribute schema docs for iterative review.

🚀

Category-by-Category

Expanded from 1 to 60+ attribute sets over 6 months. Each category validated before the next.

📈

Prompt Iteration Cycles

v1: 87% accuracy. Added few-shot examples and stricter constraints. v2: 94.2%. Tracked in Grafana.

💬

Revenue Alignment

Connected catalogue growth metrics to revenue outcomes for leadership. Data-driven scope decisions.
